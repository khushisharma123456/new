Healthy Avocado Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 small red onion
  • Cherry tomatoes
    • Images
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Chop the avocados, cucumber, and red onion.
  2. Halve the cherry tomatoes.
  3. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Toss gently and serve.
