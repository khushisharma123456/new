About Us

Bloom was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to make period care accessible, comfortable, and stress-free for everyone.

Our team of healthcare professionals, nutritionists, and mental health experts work together to provide holistic care that addresses both the physical and emotional aspects of menstrual health.

We believe in a world where periods are not a taboo subject, but a natural part of life that deserves proper attention and care.

Our app combines cutting-edge technology with evidence-based practices to offer personalized support for your unique needs.

Join the Bloom community today and transform your period experience from crampy to happy!
